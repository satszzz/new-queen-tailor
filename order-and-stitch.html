Order & Stitch from Anywhere

Want to get your outfit stitched without stepping into our store? Simply order any dress from your favorite shopping site and send it directly to our address for tailoring. We'll take care of the rest.

  • Order Online: Buy any outfit from websites like Amazon, Myntra, Meesho, Ajio, etc., and directly ship it to our store address.
  • Contact Us Immediately: After placing your order, contact us via WhatsApp to confirm delivery tracking, measurement method, and stitching plan.
  • Choose Your Measurement Type: You can:
    • Send us a perfectly fitting dress by courier for measurements
    • We collect home measurements if you're nearby (extra charge)
    • Or we will contact and guide you manually on WhatsApp
  • Payment: Full advance payment required. Cash on Delivery (COD) is not allowed.
  • Responsibility Disclaimer: Parcels must be packed securely and all risks (transit, delivery, return) are the customer's responsibility. We are not liable for any loss or damage caused by the courier.
  • Delivery Date: You will be informed about expected stitching time and delivery date after order confirmation via WhatsApp.

📦 Always share your tracking number and order screenshot with us via WhatsApp after placing your order online.
